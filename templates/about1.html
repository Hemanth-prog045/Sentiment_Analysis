About Sentiment Analysis

What is Sentiment Analysis For Mental Health Detection From Social Media Text?

Sentiment analysis is a natural language processing technique used to determine the emotional tone behind a body of text.

By leveraging Deep learning algorithms, sentiment analysis can process large volumes of social media data.

Importance of Early Detection

Early detection of mental health issues is crucial for effective treatment and support.

Sentiment analysis helps identify patterns of distress and emotional changes.

How CNN-LSTM Enhances Mental Health Detection

CNN-LSTM is a deep learning model known for speed and accuracy in analyzing text data.

It enables real-time monitoring and detection of mental health signals.
